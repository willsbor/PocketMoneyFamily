帳號刪除與資料刪除說明

Account and data deletion

如果你想移除 PocketMoney Family 帳號與相關資料，可以透過 App 內的設定頁面進行刪除。 刪除完成後，App 會回到登入畫面。

If you want to remove your PocketMoney Family account and related data, you can do so from the app settings. After deletion, the app returns to the sign-in screen.

English view. Traditional Chinese is the primary language of this site.

如何刪除

How to delete

  • 打開 App。
  • 進入設定或帳號管理頁。
  • 選擇刪除帳號或刪除小孩與所有資料。
  • 依提示完成確認。
  • Open the app.
  • Go to settings or account management.
  • Choose account deletion or delete child with all data.
  • Follow the prompts to confirm.

刪除後會發生什麼事

What happens next

  • 帳號將從系統中移除或標記為已刪除。
  • 相關家庭資料與交易資料會依後端流程清理。
  • App 介面會回到登入畫面。
  • The account is removed from the system or marked as deleted.
  • Related family and transaction data is cleaned up by the backend flow.
  • The app returns to the sign-in screen.

如果刪除遇到問題

If deletion fails

請聯絡 willsbormac@gmail.com，並提供你的登入 email 與發生時間， 方便我們確認後端刪除流程是否成功。

Please email willsbormac@gmail.com with your sign-in email and the time of the issue, so we can check whether the backend deletion flow completed successfully.
